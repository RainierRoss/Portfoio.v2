A Typical Horor Gaem

=========================================================================================
This is a 3d horror game I helped make in my graduating year of high school as a class prodject for the entire year, I was a programmer which means I was mostly just coding, taking and using the assets the 3d moduler and 2d assets gives me.

The story inside "A Typical Horor Gaem" is about the character a child waking up inside a school they wander the halls, running and avoiding the monsters in the school traveling from floor to floor to find the key to the exit once finding the key they have one last run to make avoiding all the monsters together to make it to the exit. The idea came from someone outside of the prodject because of the idea we named the player after him so he can live through the horror of the coding he inspired.

Escape Room Project
